🧠 BRAIN 2: TEXT & NAME LOGIC (CRITICAL)

OCR reads text from image:

Detected Text: "Belsiri"
Expected Text: "Bisleri"


AI applies 3 checks:

1️⃣ Exact Match Check

❌ Fails

2️⃣ Edit Distance

⚠️ Suspicious

3️⃣ Phonetic Similarity

Sounds the same
🚨 High deception risk

📌 This is a known counterfeit strategy

🧠 BRAIN 3: GENERIC AUTHENTICITY AI

Anomaly score (Autoencoder)

Example:

Anomaly Score = LOW
→ Product is well-manufactured


📌 This means:

“It is well made, BUT that doesn’t mean it is genuine.”
